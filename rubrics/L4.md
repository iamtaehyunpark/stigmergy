# Rubric L4 — Field guide with canonical terminology standard (frozen v1, before any E1 run)

Audience: engineers building LLM agents; the guide ships only if terminology is uniform.

Required elements (completeness):
1. Everything in L3: four substantive chapters, unified glossary, final assembled guide.
2. A published canonical terminology standard that is visibly the product of a survey (it names or reflects the chapter domains it standardizes), not a generic list.
3. The glossary and chapters USE the canonical terms: the standard's terms appear in the body text where their concepts are discussed.

Accuracy: as L3.
Structure: as L3, plus the standard is discoverable in the deliverable (its terms are identifiable).
Consistency (weighted heaviest at this level): no term drift - concepts referred to by exactly one canonical name across all chapters and the glossary; synonyms/aliases for the same concept in different chapters are the defining defect of this level.
